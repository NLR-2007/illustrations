# Asset Licensing & Usage Guidelines

While the core source code of **ExplainDraw** is released under the **MIT License**, the artwork, mascot references, character designs, and generated visual assets may be subject to separate terms and restrictions.

## 1. Mascot & Character Artwork
- The original mascot design is intended for the repository owner's visual identity.
- Any reference images under `references/mascot/` and `references/scenes/` are provided by the user. Do not assume these reference images are MIT licensed.
- Redistribution, commercial usage, or modification of the mascot artwork must be verified and approved by the respective rights owner.

## 2. Generated Illustrations & Visual Assets
- When generating images using external providers (such as DALL-E, Midjourney, Canva, etc.), the resulting image assets are subject to the terms of service of the respective platform.
- For commercial presentations or public distribution, users should ensure compliance with the licensing terms of their selected image generation platform.

## 3. Editable PowerPoint Diagrams
- Diagrams generated using native PowerPoint shapes and lines (via PptxGenJS) are fully editable, open, and can be used freely under the MIT License within your presentations and documents.
