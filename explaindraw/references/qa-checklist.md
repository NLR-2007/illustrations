# QA checklist

## Every output

- Pure white background
- Black linework and yellow `#FFC21A` accents only
- Clear focal idea and ample negative space
- No shadows, gradients, textures, watermarks, or accidental borders
- Correct spelling and no garbled text

## Mascot outputs

- One continuous body, not a snowman structure
- Correct oval eyes, black pupils, and two reflections per pupil
- Exactly two small yellow chest marks
- Thin arms and legs; no extra or malformed limbs
- Mascot participates in the central action

## Diagram outputs

- Correct node shapes and connections
- No overlapping nodes or clipped labels
- Decision branches are labeled and visually distinct
- Highlighting is meaningful rather than decorative
- Editable shapes remain editable when requested

## Hybrid outputs

- Illustration and diagram serve different complementary purposes
- Pane boundaries do not overlap
- Shared title, palette, and visual rhythm
- Illustration is rendered rather than left as an unexplained placeholder
