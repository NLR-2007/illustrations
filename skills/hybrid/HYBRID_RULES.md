# Hybrid Mode Rules (HYBRID_RULES.md)

Guidelines for organizing space and content on hybrid presentation slides.

---

## 1. Grid Divisions (16:9 widescreen)

To maintain balance, split the slide using standard coordinates:

- **Horizontal Split**:
  - Left Section (Illustration): `X: 0.8`, `Y: 1.8`, `W: 5.5`, `H: 4.8` inches.
  - Right Section (Diagram): `X: 7.0`, `Y: 1.8`, `W: 5.5`, `H: 4.8` inches.
  - Middle Gap: `0.7 inches` of pure white space to prevent visual crowding.

- **Vertical Split**:
  - Top Section (Illustration): `X: 0.8`, `Y: 1.8`, `W: 11.7`, `H: 2.2` inches.
  - Bottom Section (Diagram): `X: 0.8`, `Y: 4.3`, `W: 11.7`, `H: 2.3` inches.
  - Vertical Gap: `0.3 inches` of white space.

---

## 2. Text Alignment & Sizing

- **Title**: Left-aligned, placed at `X: 0.8`, `Y: 0.5`. Font size `24pt`, color `#111111`.
- **Labels**: Subheadings for both sections should be placed at the top of each block (`Y: 1.4` or `Y: 4.0`), using `14pt` bold text.
- **Subtext**: Descriptions under the illustration or diagram should be in `10pt` secondary gray (`#666666`).

---

## 3. Style Harmony

- The illustration image must be surrounded by a thin light gray border (`#E8E8E8`) or have no border (floating on the pure white slide background).
- The diagram elements on the other side must use the exact same black (`#111111`) outlines and yellow (`#FFC21A`) accent highlight colors.
- Avoid placing busy illustrations next to complex diagrams. If the diagram has more than 6 nodes, simplify the illustration concept.
