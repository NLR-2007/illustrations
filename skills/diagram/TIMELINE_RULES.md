# Timeline & Roadmap Rules

Rules for rendering chronological timelines and roadmaps on 16:9 slides.

---

## 1. Timeline Structure

Timelines are structured as horizontal flows:

- **Central Axis**: A thick horizontal black connector line running from the left margin to the right margin through the vertical center (`Y: 3.75` inches).
- **Milestone Nodes**: Rectangles or rounded rectangles containing the event or project phase name.
- **Alternating Spacing**: To fit more content without clutter, milestone shapes are placed alternately:
  - Phase 1: Above the line.
  - Phase 2: Below the line.
  - Phase 3: Above the line, and so on.
- **Connectors**: Vertical dashed lines (`#666666`) connecting each milestone node to its corresponding point on the central horizontal axis.

---

## 2. Roadmap Layouts

Roadmaps are formatted as horizontal swimlanes or block chains:

- **Swimlanes**: Horizontal bars marking different tracks (e.g. Frontend, Backend, Marketing).
- **Blocks**: Linked rectangles within each track representing phases (Q1, Q2, Q3) with arrows pointing right.
- **Fills**:
  - Upcoming milestone: White background `#FFFFFF`, black border.
  - Current milestone: Yellow background `#FFC21A` to highlight focus.
  - Completed milestone: Light gray background `#E8E8E8`.
